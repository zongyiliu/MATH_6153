10. Let $B$ be a standard $\left(\mathcal{F}_{t}\right)$-Brownian motion on $\left(\Omega, \mathcal{F},\left(\mathcal{F}_{t}\right), \mathbb{P}\right)$ with $B_{0}=1$. Let $\tau_{x}$ denote the first hitting time of $x$, and for $n \geq 1$ set

$$
\mathbb{Q}_{n}(\cdot)=\mathbb{P}\left(\cdot \mid \tau_{n}<\tau_{0}\right)
$$

(a) $[3 \mathrm{pts}]$ Evaluate $M_{t}^{(n)}=\mathbb{E}_{\mathbb{P}}\left(\mathbf{1}_{\tau_{n}<\tau_{0}} \mid \mathcal{F}_{t}\right)$.\\[0pt]
(b) [4 pts] Find a function $b_{n}:(0, \infty) \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ such that, under $\mathbb{Q}_{n}$, there is a standard Brownian motion $\beta$ such that

$$
